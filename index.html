My name is Gaurav Mehta, I am a Photo Manipulation Artist and Travelling Photographer. I have been Practicing Photo Manipulation since past 4 years and Practicing Photography since past 2 years.

About Me

I am a Graphic Designer by profession and Photographer, Writer, Traveller by passion. As of my work experience I have worked with Decathlon for 3 years. In 3 years I have worked as Sport leader, Delhi Design Leader and Mountain Sports Communication Leader India .I have gained my experience in Merchandising, Customer handling and Brand strategies. Currently I am working as Freelance Graphic Designer, I provide Designing solutions to retail stores and coordinate with printers for smooth work flow.
My trajectory is to be a Full time Travelling Photographer and I have set a goal to cover different Festivals across India in next 4 years.

Photo Manipulation

Photo manipulation is what brought me close to photography, I started with random images and slowly I created my own style. Most of my manipulation art is inspired from anime and I always try to introuce action or momentum in my frame to pass a story or a message.
